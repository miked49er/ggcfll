About First LEGO League

FLL Core Values

Inspire the young people to become science and technology leader by engaging them in programs that builds self-confidence, knowledge and life skills.


  • We are a team.
  • We do the work to find solutions with guidance from our coaches and mentors
  • We know our coaches and mentors don't have all the answers; we learn together.
  • We honor the spirit of friendly competition.
  • What we discover is more important than what we win.
  • We share our experiences with others.
  • We display Gracious Professionalism® and Coopertition® in everything we do.
  • We have FUN!

The Competition

The competition is split into four distinct parts:


  • Research Project and 5 min. presentation - 30%
  • Technical Design - 30%
  • Teamwork and a 10 minute judgment- 30%
  • Robot Game Score timed at two minutes and thirty seconds - 10%

The Season

The season begins in August and goes until the first week of February


  • December: Regional competion
  • January: SuperRegional competion
  • Last Saturday of January and first Saturday of February: State Championship Competion
