Registration

Registering for the GGC First Lego League team is twofold:

First you must register with the national First Lego League
Registration includes a robot kit and a Challenge Set. New teams can expect to pay approximately $900 for team registration (250$), a robot kit of parts (500$), and a Challenge Set (formerly known as the Field Setup Kit)(75$). Additional costs for event participation, travel, food, team shirts and other optional items will vary. Veteran teams will pay less since the robot set can be used year after year. Registration is open from May-September and takes teams of up to 10 members, ages 9-14 in grades 4-8.

Secondly, register for the Georgia First Lego Legaue competition.
The registration period for this is the first week of October and costs 75$.
